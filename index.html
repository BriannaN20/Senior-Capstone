Hidden Chemicals in Your Food

A research project by Brianna & Kim

The Problem

Every day, millions of people consume packaged snacks without realizing the synthetic chemicals and preservatives hidden in the ingredients list. Our research aims to shed light on what these additives actually do to our bodies and why "clean eating" has become a necessity in the modern market.

Common Chemicals to Watch

Community Voices: Healthcare Interviews

To connect our literary research with real-world clinical observations, we coordinated and conducted interviews with medical professionals. They provided invaluable field insights regarding the metabolic and pediatric trends they see daily.

Clinical Interview 1

Key Insight: Misleading labels on processed foods mask hidden ingredients, causing significant health issues when consumers unknowingly purchase and eat unhealthy options.

Clinical Interview 2

Key Insight: Modern rates of diabetes and hypertension are closely linked to hyper-processed food consumption, affecting roughly half of all patients observed in clinical practice.

Clinical Interview 3

Key Insight: Artificial sweeteners carry risks comparable to refined sugars, while excessive consumption of sugar and salt remains a primary driver of major health complications.
